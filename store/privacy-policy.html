Privacy Policy — Rybbit analytics (Unofficial)

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Rybbit analytics (Unofficial) (package com.nkshub.rybbit_unofficial) is an unofficial, open-source mobile client for the Rybbit web analytics platform. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Rybbit team. This policy explains what the app does with your data.

Data you provide

  • Server URL of your Rybbit instance.
  • Authentication: your email and password, or an API key.

These are stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage (Keystore-backed flutter_secure_storage, with a local fallback). They are never sent to us or to any third party. They are transmitted only to the Rybbit server you configure, in order to sign in and fetch your analytics.

Network

The app makes network requests only to the Rybbit server URL you enter. The developer of this app operates no backend and receives none of your analytics data or credentials.

Crash reporting

If the app was built with a Sentry DSN, anonymous crash and error diagnostics may be sent to a self-hosted Sentry instance to help fix bugs. This contains technical diagnostic data only (stack traces, device model, OS version) and no credentials or analytics content. Builds without a configured DSN send nothing.

What we do NOT do

  • No advertising and no ad identifiers.
  • No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app, or signing out, removes all stored credentials from your device. There is no server-side account held by the app developer to delete.
